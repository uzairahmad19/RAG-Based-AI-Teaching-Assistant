LectureLens
Final Year Project · 2025
Video Intelligence System

Ask anything about
your lecture videos

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline that transcribes, indexes, and semantically searches lecture content — guiding you to the exact timestamp where your answer lives.

// system architecture
01 · Stage
Audio Extraction
FFmpeg strips audio tracks from MP4 lectures into structured MP3 files.
process_video.py
02 · Stage
Transcription
Faster-Whisper converts speech to timed text segments (chunks) in JSON.
create_chunks.py
03 · Stage
Embedding
BGE-M3 encodes each chunk into a semantic vector and persists to disk.
read_chunks.py
04 · Stage
RAG Query
Cosine similarity retrieves top-K chunks; LLaMA 3.2 synthesises the answer.
process_query.py
